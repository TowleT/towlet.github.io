Who We Are

About Oxford Bean Publishing


Oxford Bean Publishing is an independent literary press founded to champion writing that refuses compromise — books with roots in the physical world, the open road, and the long tradition of writers who chose experience over safety.

We are a small press with a deliberately small list. We publish fewer books than the industry expects, and we give each one the time, craft, and attention it deserves. Our ISBN registry, editorial rigour, and distribution through Ingram ensure that our authors reach the widest possible readership.

We believe that the best books are written by people who have actually lived what they're writing about. That is the foundation of every publishing decision we make.

I Editorial Rigour

Every manuscript receives deep editorial attention. We do not rush books to market. We publish when a book is ready.

II Author Partnership

We work with writers, not for them. Authors are partners in every decision from design to distribution to publicity.

III Crafted Design

Cover design, interior typography, and paper stock are treated as integral to the reading experience, not afterthoughts.

IV Wide Distribution

Full Ingram distribution. All titles fully returnable. Available through all major retailers and independent bookshops worldwide.
